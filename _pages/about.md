I am a PhD student in Computer Science at [Arizona State University](https://www.asu.edu/) (January 2026 – present). I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from the [University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)](https://en.ustc.edu.cn/) in May 2025, and my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from USTC in May 2022.

My research interests lie in **Deep Learning** and **Machine Learning**, with a particular focus on Large Language Models (LLMs). I have experience in LoRA finetuning, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), contrastive learning, and prompt learning. My applied work spans supply chain decision-making with LLMs and sentiment-controlled text summarization.

I completed an NLP internship at [Iflytek](https://www.iflytek.com/en/), Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Hefei in October 2022. I have been awarded the USTC Scholarship three times (Autumn 2022, Spring 2023, Autumn 2023).
